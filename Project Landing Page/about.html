About Me

I’m naturally a creative and hands on type of person. I grew up playing the piano and composing my own music. I’m also a member of the United States Air Force Reserves Civil Engineering unit at Dobbin Air Reserve Base in Georgia where I build everything from sidewalks to buildings. After serving 13 years with the Air Force on Active Duty my job was downsized and I was forced to leave. It was in that moment where I decided I needed a new career in a field with high demand and great job security. I searched hard and long and found my way into the technology field where I’m enjoying every moment as a developer. I love programming and coding. I’m always self-learning and trying find new and faster ways to build and create my projects. Please view my portfolio to see examples of my skills as a web developer using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, MVC and .Net. I am very thorough, creative and I love to collaborate with other likeminded individuals to create outstanding product.

Family

My greatest treasure in this world is my family. I love spending time with them and making fun and lasting memeories. Everything I do is for the betterment of my family. My family is the motivation behind my dedication to be the very best I can.
