About Me

My journey into software development began in 2015 after serving 13 years with the United States Air Force. I have always had an interest in computers so when the time came for me to choose a new career path I went full speed ahead into software development. I had no prior experience with software development however I was very determined to learn. I began learning C# and HTML and in the process discovered that I truly enjoyed what I was doing. Programming has proven itself to be challenging but the rewards are very fulfilling. I am highly motivated to be a developer because of my natural interest in computers and my desire to make a positive difference in the lives of others with the creative power of computing.

A fun fact about myself is that I’m naturally a creative and hand-on type of person. I taught myself how to play the piano and organ at the age of 12 years old. I composed my own music and have been a church musician.

Aside from programming, my other two passions are music and gardening. I've been playing the piano for 21 years (off and on) and I've been gardening slightly longer than 21 years. If I'm not spending time on programming, then I'm likely keying through notes on my piano or outside maintaining my garden. If you'd like to know more about anything presented on this website, feel free to contact me.

Thank you for your time.

My greatest treasure in this world is my family. I love spending time with them and making fun and lasting memeories. Everything I do is for my family. Making my family proud of me is the motivation behind my dedication to be the very best I can.
